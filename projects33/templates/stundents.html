STUDY PROGRAMME

At Sri Chaitanya, we believe in a stress-free learning environment. To that end, our curriculum is specifically designed to lift the burden off students’ shoulders, ensuring that learning is an interactive, engaging process. Special emphasis is put on effective and enjoyable methods of education (our digital learning initiatives are one such example) so that students become knowledgeable, inquisitive global learners

Personality Development

A well-rounded personality is built on the back of all-round grooming. Being the future citizens of a diverse country, it is essential that these children uphold our rich cultural values. We endeavour to cultivate these values within our global learners through cultural meets, art competitions, music and dance competitions, and much more. At Sri Chaitanya, every child is given a platform to hone and enhance their talents, pursuing them simultaneously with academics.

DIGITAL LEARNING

To usher in a new era of world-class education, SHREE BASAVESHWAR GURUKUL SCHOOL has adopted a technology-driven approach to studies. The schools have replaced textbooks with trendy tabs for students from class five to seven. This learning technique not only makes children adept in the use of technology but also reduce the load of study materials to be carried every day.

HEALTH SERVICE

A healthy mind and body is a must for a successful life. Keeping this in mind, SHREE BASAVESHWAR GURUKUL SCHOOL provides its students with a nutritious and well-balanced diet. Apart from this, the schools boast of an in-house dispensary for round-the-clock medical attention. Experienced doctors visit the schools twice a day to attend to ailing children. Light freehand exercises, sports, meditation and yoga are an essential part of our students’ everyday lives, ensuring holistic wellness.

NATURAL DELIGHTS

SHREE BASAVESHWAR GURUKUL SCHOOL green campus is truly eco-friendly. Scores of trees, landscaped gardens, and ample fresh air create a tranquil atmosphere around the schools. Owing to a healthy environment, the students are active, healthy and are able to perform at their best. Activities like rock climbing, trekking and hiking are undertaken to help children be one with nature throughout their school life

MODERN CAMPUS

State-of-the-art infrastructure and modern amenities are a trademark of all SHREE BASAVESHWAR GURUKUL SCHOOL. Schools in all major towns and cities have sophisticated residential campuses. Some of the salient features include spacious dormitories, large and ultramodern dining halls, hostels with individual lockers, air-conditioned classrooms, full-time hostel wardens, and much more. The children at the boarding house are taken care of by a concerned and watchful warden, whose guidance and atention makes the children feel at home.
