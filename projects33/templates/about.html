ABOUT US

Shri Channabasaveshwar Gurukul was established in 1992-93 with 40 students and 2 teachers. It was founded by Dr.Basavalinga Pattaddevaru in Hiremathasamsthana Bhalki with only 500/- in his hand. The aim of the school is “Providing a good quality education along with Samskara (combination of traditional, cultural and spiritual skills)”. Later on it was shifted near Karadyal which is of about 50 acres vast campus beside Bidar-Bhalki Now the campus is shining with 1 to 10th both residential & day school, PU science, D.Ed. & B.Ed. colleges.

Our Mission

The school & colleges in the campus are fully equipped with necessary infrastructures to cater the needs of students. The campus has a fine team of qualified, experienced & dedicated staff members, wide play grounds, a library with huge number of books, Bus facility & 24 hours hospital facility. Today this is the only campus with Orphans care Centre (Anathashrama) in Gulbarga division.
